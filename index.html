About Coca Cola

Coca-Cola is the most popular and biggest-selling soft drink in history, as well as the best-known product in the world. Created in 1886 in Atlanta, Georgia, by Dr. John S. Pemberton, Coca-Cola was first offered as a fountain beverage at Jacob's Pharmacy by mixing Coca-Cola syrup with carbonated water.
